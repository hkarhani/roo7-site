When will I receive my commission? +

Commissions are paid monthly after your referral maintains an active subscription for 30 days. Payments are processed on the 1st of each month.

How much can I earn per referral? +

You earn 20% of the first year subscription fee. For Tier 1 ($500/year), you earn $100. For Tier 2 (8% of portfolio), commissions vary based on portfolio size.

Is there a limit to how many people I can refer? +

No limit! You can refer as many people as you want. The more successful referrals, the more you earn.

If a referral cancels within 30 days, the commission is not paid. If they cancel after 30 days, you keep the commission already earned.
