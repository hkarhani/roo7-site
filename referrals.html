❓ Frequently Asked Questions

When will I receive my commission? +

Commissions are paid immediately when a referred customer uses your code during invoice payment. No waiting periods required.

How much can I earn per referral? +

You earn 20% commission on each invoice payment where your code is used. For $99/month Tier 1, you earn $19.80 per payment. For higher tiers ($299/$999), commissions scale accordingly.

Is there a limit to how many people I can refer? +

No limit! You can refer as many people as you want. The more successful referrals, the more you earn.

What happens if my referral cancels their subscription? +

You keep all commissions already earned from payments where your code was used. Future payments without your code won't generate commissions.
