1. üè¶ Creating a New Trading Account

To start automated trading, you need to connect your exchange account to ROO7. This process is secure and allows ROO7 to execute trades on your behalf.

Step-by-Step Process:

  1. On your dashboard, click the "+ Add New Account" button in the Live Accounts section
  2. Enter a descriptive Account Name (e.g., "My Binance SPOT Account")
  3. Select your Exchange (currently Binance is supported)
  4. Choose your Account Type:
    • SPOT: For regular cryptocurrency trading
    • FUTURES: For futures trading (coming soon)
  5. Set up your Exchange API credentials (see below)
  6. Click "Save Account" to create your account

Setting Up Exchange API Keys (Binance Example):

  1. Log into your Binance account
  2. Go to API Management in your account settings
  3. Click "Create API" and choose "System generated"
  4. Enter a label like "ROO7 Trading Bot"
  5. Complete security verification (2FA, email, etc.)
  6. Enable "Enable Spot & Margin Trading" permission
  7. Copy your API Key and Secret Key
  8. Paste both keys into the ROO7 form
üîí Security Important: Never share your API keys with anyone else. ROO7 stores them securely and encrypted. For additional security, consider setting up IP restrictions on your exchange API settings.
üí° Tip: You can use the same API credentials for multiple accounts (SPOT and FUTURES) by clicking the "Use Same API Credentials" button when creating additional accounts.

2. üîß Troubleshooting Your Account

If your account isn't working properly, use the built-in troubleshoot tool to diagnose and fix common issues.

How to Access Troubleshooting:

  1. In your Live Accounts table, find the account with issues
  2. Click the blue wrench icon (üîß) in the Actions column
  3. This opens the troubleshoot window with your account details

Troubleshooting Process:

  1. Account Information Review: Check that your account name, exchange, and type are correct
  2. API Connection Test: Click "Test API Connection" to verify your credentials
  3. Balance Verification: Ensure your exchange account has sufficient balance
  4. Permission Check: Verify your API key has the necessary trading permissions
  5. Error Log Review: Check any error messages for specific issues

Common Issues and Solutions:

‚ùå API Connection Failed

Check your API key and secret are correct. Verify the API key isn't expired or revoked on your exchange.

‚ö†Ô∏è Insufficient Balance

Deposit more funds to your exchange account or reduce your hedge percentage to free up capital.

üîí Permission Denied

Enable "Spot Trading" permissions on your exchange API key settings.

üåê Network Issues

Check your internet connection and try refreshing the page. Exchange servers may be temporarily down.

3. ‚öôÔ∏è Updating Account Settings

You can modify your account settings at any time to adjust risk management and account status.

Accessing Account Settings:

  1. In your Live Accounts table, find the account to modify
  2. Click the yellow settings icon (‚öôÔ∏è) in the Actions column
  3. This opens the Account Settings modal

Available Settings:

üõ°Ô∏è Hedge Percentage

  • Purpose: Determines what percentage of your account stays in USDT (cash)
  • Range: 0% to 100%
  • Example: 20% hedge means 80% is invested, 20% stays as USDT
  • Higher hedge: More conservative, less risk
  • Lower hedge: More aggressive, higher exposure

Account Status Options:

‚úÖ Active Account

Normal operation - trading is enabled and strategies execute automatically.

‚è∏Ô∏è Disable Account

Temporarily stops trading while keeping your positions. You can re-enable anytime.

üî¥ Revoke Access

Liquidates all positions to USDT and then disables the account permanently.

‚ö†Ô∏è Warning: "Revoke Access" will sell all your holdings and convert them to USDT. This action cannot be undone easily - only use this if you want to completely stop trading and cash out.

Making Changes:

  1. Adjust the hedge percentage using the number input field
  2. Toggle account status switches as needed
  3. Click "Update Account" to save changes
  4. Changes take effect within a few minutes

4. üìä Assigning Trading Strategies

ROO7 offers multiple trading strategies to match your risk tolerance and investment goals.

Available Strategies:

üî• HRR (High Risk Return)

Aggressive strategy targeting higher returns with increased volatility. Best for risk-tolerant investors.

‚öñÔ∏è MRR (Medium Risk Return)

Balanced approach with moderate risk and steady growth potential. Good for most investors.

üõ°Ô∏è LRR (Low Risk Return)

Conservative strategy focusing on capital preservation with lower volatility.

üéØ Custom Portfolio

Create your own asset allocation with specific cryptocurrencies and weightings.

Assigning a Strategy:

  1. In your Live Accounts table, find the account you want to configure
  2. Click the green strategy icon (üìà) in the Actions column
  3. Review the account details displayed in the modal
  4. Select your desired strategy from the dropdown menu
  5. Configure strategy parameters if available
  6. Click "Assign Strategy" to activate

Creating a Custom Portfolio:

  1. Select "Custom Portfolio" from the strategy dropdown
  2. Click "+ Add Instrument" to add cryptocurrencies
  3. Enter the Symbol (e.g., BTC, ETH, ADA) for each asset
  4. Set the Weight % for each asset (must total 100%)
  5. The system validates your portfolio in real-time
  6. Remove unwanted assets using the red ‚ùå button
  7. Once validated, click "Assign Strategy"

Rebalancing Frequency:

Some strategies offer rebalancing options:

  • Daily: Portfolio rebalances every day
  • Weekly: Portfolio rebalances every week
  • Monthly: Portfolio rebalances monthly
  • Manual: Only rebalances when you request it
üí° Tip: You can change strategies at any time. The system will gradually transition your holdings from the old strategy to the new one.

  2. Click "Connect Wallet"

  4. Select the preferred cryptocurrency for payments
  5. Verify the wallet address carefully
  6. Click "Save Wallet" to confirm

üìù Sign-up Bonus

6. üìÑ Invoice & Subscription Process

To activate automated trading, you need to subscribe to ROO7's service. This process involves generating an invoice and making payment.

Accessing the Invoice System:

  1. From your dashboard, click "üìÑ Invoices" in the header
  2. This opens the invoice management page

Complete Invoice Process:

Step 1: Generate Invoice

  1. On the invoices page, click "Generate Invoice"
  2. The system calculates pricing based on your connected accounts
  3. Review the invoice details and pricing tier
  4. The invoice PDF is generated automatically

Step 2: Apply Referral Code (Optional)

  1. If you have a referral code, enter it in the "Referral Code" field
  2. Click "Apply Code" to validate
  3. Your discount will be applied to the total amount
  4. The updated invoice reflects the discounted price

Step 3: Activate Account & Get Payment Details

  1. Click "Activate Account" button
  2. The system provides cryptocurrency wallet address for payment
  3. Note the exact amount to send and the wallet address
  4. Payment instructions include supported cryptocurrencies

Step 4: Make Payment

  1. Open your cryptocurrency wallet or exchange
  2. Send the exact amount to the provided wallet address
  3. Use the specified cryptocurrency (usually USDT)
  4. Double-check the wallet address before confirming
  5. Complete the transaction

Step 5: Automatic Trading Activation

  1. ROO7 monitors for your payment automatically
  2. Once payment is confirmed on the blockchain, your subscription activates
  3. You'll receive confirmation via email and dashboard notification
  4. Automated trading begins within 24 hours of payment confirmation
  5. Your account status changes to "Active Subscription"

  • Enterprise: Large portfolios, premium support
